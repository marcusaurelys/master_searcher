\begin{frame}{Why Bird-BM Can Win Comparisons but Lose Runtime}
\small
\begin{itemize}
  \item In this implementation, Bird-family preprocessing and traversal overhead is significant.
  \item At larger texts/patterns, that overhead becomes non-negligible.
  \item Result: Bird-BM has lowest comparisons (\HeadCompBirdBM{}) but higher runtime (\HeadTimeBirdBM{} s).
  \item Greedy BM remains faster in runtime terms (\HeadTimeGreedyBM{} s) despite higher comparisons.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
